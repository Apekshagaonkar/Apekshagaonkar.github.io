About Me

I am an aspiring Data Scientist who is passionate about solving problems and enjoys extracting insights from data to make the product work better and enhance the business.
Since my childhood, I have always been a curious kid who was into analyzing surrounding and building own theory behind situations and engineering small things: Be it a worn out wire in speakers or burnt board with CPU and eventually I fell in love with gadgets and as a result, I took electronics as my subject in 11th and 12th and later carried on my studies in Electronics and Communications as my field of study in my Bachelor's degree.
In my engineering degree, I had an opportunity to build one simple soccer robot in Freshman year and that fascinated me into robotics . So following that I built a Fruit harvesting prototype robot and in the process of building that project I got the opportunity to learn about machine learning.
And that was the beginning of my data science and Neural network journey and Since then i have been constantly learning new things that has blown my mind and kept me eager to learn more about it.
Apart from my love towards technology I love spending time in nature,traveling to new places ,talking to new people and understanding new perspectives and writing .
